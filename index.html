Hello, Welcome to my packing planner!

I designed this page for people like myself who LOVE travelling but HATE packing, and that nagging feeling you might have forgotten something essential on the journey to the airport.
This page grants you the ability to pre plan your bag and check off items as you pack them, preventing that dredded feeling and allowing you to travel with the assurance that you have everything you need.
